Basic Privacy Measures for Browsing the Internet

  • Search Engine: DuckDuckGo
  • Browser: Firefox or Tor Browser
  • Browser Extenstions: HTTPS Everywhere, uBlock Origin, NoScript
  • Best (Free) VPN: ProtonVPN, OpenVPN
  • Best Password Manager: RoboForm
